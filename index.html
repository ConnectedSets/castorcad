Architectural Visualization

Do you have a 2 dimensional plan of your house, hotel, or apartment? We can make your project come to life by creating a 3 dimensional model of your building in just a couple days. We provide high quality images of any part of your project whether it be exterior or interior. This increases your chances of getting a final outcome the way you want it to look in the real world without spending money and additional time on renovations.

Efficiency

We are very serious about delivering our products with no delay while reaching our goal with our clients. To keep our word on how long a project may take we will need your help in providing us with all the information we require. This will help you and us to get your desired outcome within your deadline. CastorCAD is here to make sure you can see what your building will look like before you make any changes to the construction site which would be too expensive to change once done.

Architectural Animation

Our services do not stop with just architectural image visualization. We can also provide videos/animations to our clients who would require such a service. This is the best way to make your project come to life for a reasonable price. Using this service will almost always save you money because most still images you require will be provided free of charge.
